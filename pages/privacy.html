PRIVACY FRAMEWORK

Your coordinate data privacy remains central to our luxury curating standards.

1. Data Collection Protocols

We compile client parameters (Full Name, Phone Coordinates, Email Addresses, and Preferred Properties selections) exclusively to coordinate private property viewings, execute mortgage calculation overlays, and log CRM leads inbox requests. No details are distributed to third-party databases.

2. Security & Local Storage Sync

Wishlist records and compared parameters are saved locally in the browser's `localStorage` cache. Users retain absolute privilege to clear cache indexes or archive active viewing requests in the CMS dashboard at any time.

3. Framework Compliance

LuxeHaven operates in compliance with international privacy protocols including GDPR and CCPA, maintaining strict security wrappers for all transacted leads and client communications.
